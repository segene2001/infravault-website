Unified Infrastructure Platform

InfraVault Systems is more than a collection of tools. It is a unified platform for collecting, indexing, and acting on infrastructure data across your estate.

Unified Data Collectors

Leverage SNMP, SSH/CLI, and modern APIs to discover devices, capture configurations, and learn controller state from platforms like APIC, Panorama, and FortiManager.

Secure Credential & RBAC Model

Centralize device access with a credential model designed for least privilege, coupled with role-based access control for operations, security, and audit teams.

Jobs, Scheduling & Audit

A common job engine powers inventory, backup, compliance checks, and remediation. Every run is tracked with trigger metadata and audit logs so you always know who did what, and when.

Products

Start with the core building blocks for network reliability: authoritative inventory and safe configuration backup & compliance.

ConfigVault — Backup & Compliance

Automated configuration backup, history, diff, and safe rollback for routers, switches, firewalls, and controllers.

  • Nightly backups across multi-vendor networks.
  • Snapshot history with unified diff and change summary.
  • Last Known Good markers and one-click restore for supported platforms.
  • Lightweight compliance rules for AAA, logging, NTP, SNMP, and APIC tenants.
  • Operator-driven remediation workflows with full audit trail.
Learn more about ConfigVault →

NetVault — Network Inventory

Accurate, searchable inventory for your network devices, built for operations, security, and lifecycle teams.

  • Central view of hostname, management IP, vendor, platform, OS, and model.
  • Track last inventory and backup timestamps per device.
  • Highlight devices that are stale, missing backups, or inactive.
  • Expose EOS/EOL status for device families to support lifecycle planning and risk management.
  • Provide inventory and coverage evidence for audits and change reviews.
