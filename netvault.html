NetVault — Authoritative Network Inventory

NetVault gives you a single, reliable view of network devices across vendors, with lifecycle insight and coverage evidence for operations, security, and audit teams.

Request NetVault Demo See Inventory Model

Why NetVault

  • Cut through spreadsheets and ad-hoc CMDB entries.
  • Quickly answer basic questions about what is deployed where.
  • Support change, security reviews, and lifecycle planning from one source of truth.

Inventory Model

NetVault focuses on the data points that matter most for day-to-day operations and lifecycle decisions.

Per-Device View

  • Hostname and management IP.
  • Vendor, platform, OS, and model.
  • Tags or roles to group devices by environment or function.
  • Last inventory timestamp.
  • Last backup timestamp when paired with ConfigVault.

Signals for Action

NetVault highlights devices that are stale, missing backups, or not being inventoried as expected, so teams can prioritize attention where it is needed most.

EOS/EOL & Lifecycle Insight

End-of-Life Awareness

NetVault is designed to surface EOS/EOL status per device family, giving teams a clear view of lifecycle risk across the estate.

This allows planning hardware refresh, budgeting, and risk mitigation work in a more structured way.

Planning & Governance

When paired with backup and compliance data from ConfigVault, NetVault helps connect lifecycle status to operational readiness, giving a more complete picture for governance and change boards.

Coverage & Evidence

NetVault is built to help you answer audit and due-diligence questions quickly.

Operational Coverage

Understand which devices are being inventoried, which have recent backups, and where there may be gaps in visibility.

Audit-Ready Data

Provide evidence of inventory, backup coverage, and lifecycle posture using a consistent, exportable data model.

Working with ConfigVault

Complementary Roles

NetVault answers "what and where" for devices, while ConfigVault provides the configuration backup and compliance lens. Together they give a fuller view of network health.

Shared Platform Services

Both products run on the InfraVault platform, sharing RBAC, jobs, audit logging, and credential management, so teams have one consistent operational model.
